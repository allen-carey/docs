## Introduction

The <a class="endtest_link" href="https://app.endtest.io/drive">Drive</a> section allows you to upload files that you can use in your `Web Tests` and `Mobile Tests`.

## Guide

After you upload a file, you can configure the Visibility to have one of the following values:

* Only me
* My entire team

You can copy the `File Download URL` and use it in your tests.

The uploaded files will be shown in an autocomplete dropdown in the `File URL` input from the `Upload File` action.

And the `IPA` and `APK` files will be shown in an autocomplete dropdown in the `APK Download URL` and `IPA Download URL` inputs from the `Run Test Suite` modal for `Mobile Tests`.
